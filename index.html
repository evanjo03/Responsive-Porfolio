About Me

My name is John Evans, and I grew up in the greater Madison, Wisconsin area. I always had a passion for numbers and thoroughly enjoyed my early mathematics classes in highschool. I attended Luther College in Iowa, and studied math theory and applications there. While enrolled at Luther, I participated in a few different clubs, including math club, and also played on the school's ultimate frisbee team.

After graduation, I moved with a few fellow Luther grads to the Twin Cities area, settling into the suburbs to the south while looking for a job. I ended up working in financial services for the global chemical company, Ecolab, and worked in data mining and work order management for corporate customers, which is where I currently work. While I have learned a lot working at Ecolab, I believe my career lies outside the realm of finance. I am hoping to move into the tech industry to work in the software development field, data analytics, or similar, and cannot wait to see what opportunities await!
